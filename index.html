backOUT

backOut is a story game, inspired by black out poetry and the choose your own adventure genre of games, about emphasis and what redaction means. The protagonist is a young woman who has made a mistake and hopes to correct it, magically; to play, choose from the available terms what she should be focusing on.

INSTRUCTIONS

Note that the instructions below pertain to the full version of the game. Not there yet! For now, please enjoy the ability to make a game.

backOut is a game about emphasis. To begin, click the games link above, and create a new story.

You'll be faced with a sentence, that involves a choice. Two words can be clicked, to shift emphasis. Click one to proceed, and choose again.

When you make choices to make the protagonist more hopeful that she can fix her mistake, your hope grows.

When you make choices that increase either the protagonist's acceptance of the situation, or her knowledge of it, your wisdom grows.

When she gives up, the game is over.
